Controls

Your "Submarine" will follow your mouse. Click to fire, but beware you have limited ammo. To gain ammo, as well as health, speed, and oxygen run into the colored "bubble" power-ups. Your oxygen will go down as you play so make sure you get enough oxygen power-ups or you will lose control and float to the surface (where your oxygen will then refill). Good Luck!
